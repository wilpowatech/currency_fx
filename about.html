About This Project

The Kwame Currency Exchange project is a smart financial solution built to aid recognition and conversion of currencies using computer vision and artificial intelligence techniques.

It supports five major currencies: Nigerian Naira (NGN), US Dollar (USD), Euro (EUR), Australian Dollar (AUD), and Indian Rupee (INR).

Through image processing, the system recognizes currency types and values, then connects to live exchange rate APIs to convert between currencies in real-time.

The project leverages HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and deep learning concepts such as Feedforward and Time Delay Neural Networks (TDNN).
